Description

PUBG also known as "PlayerUknown's Battlegrounds" is a Battle Royal, a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch where players fight to remain the last alive. The game is published in March 2017 by "PUBG corporation", a South Korean company. As a typical game of it's genre, each match starts with players parachuting from a plane onto one of the four maps, with areas of approximately Erangel and Miramar-8 × 8 kilometres, Vikendi-6 × 6 kilometres, and Sanhok-4 × 4 kilometres in size. After they land, players have to search buildings, towns and other to find weapons, vehicles, armor, and other equipment. Killed players can be looted as well. Every few minutes, the playable area of the map begins to shrink down , with any player caught outside the safe area taking damage incrementally. The last team or player that is still alive wins the match.
